Pay What You Want Game Bundles (5):


Bundle In A Box is a thematic pay-what-you-want gaming bundle, that helps new games debut, supports charity and directly strengthens creators via the Indie Dev Grant. As more people buy it more extras get unlocked and more money is donated.



Groupees runs unique sales on music, games, ebooks and films- benefiting charities around the world.



Indiegala lets customers set their own price for high-quality indie games and music, then donate some or all of the proceeds to Able Gamers and Italian Red Cross for helping Emilia hit by earthquake.



Indie Royale is an indie game bundle website that offers up multiple top-quality indie titles every two weeks for you to purchase at seriously silly prices. All you need to do is pay the price stated - or pay more - and the games currently featured will be yours to keep forever.



Humble Bundle is a series of collections ("bundles") of books, games, and music that are sold and distributed online at a price determined by the purchaser and buyers can set the revenue split between the developers, charities and humble bundle organizers.
