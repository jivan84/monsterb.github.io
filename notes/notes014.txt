What OpenBSD version are you running?



1.) You can use commands "uname -mrs" or "sysctl kern.version".

  # uname -mrs

  or

  # sysctl kern.version

Output:

OpenBSD 6.0 amd64

or

kern.version=OpenBSD 6.0 (GENERIC.MP) #2319: Tue Jul 26 13:00:43 MDT 2016
    deraadt@amd64.openbsd.org:/usr/src/sys/arch/amd64/compile/GENERIC.MP
