Title: Upload your Garmin Forerunner 10 activities on Linux.
Date: August 13, 2017
URL: https://monsterb.github.io/notes/notes015.txt
License: http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/
Author: monsterb

- Plug-in your Garmin Forerunner 10 to your Linux box. It mounts as a USB file system.
- Go to https://connect.garmin.com/.
- Log in.
- Click on "Activities".
- On the right, above all of your activities, click the "Import" link.
- Go to the Garmin USB deivce.
- Go into the the "Activities" folder.
- Select the most recent .fit file and upload.
