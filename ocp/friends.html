Friends of Oggcast Planet:


  • Finux Tech Weekly is hosted by Arron Finnon (aka finux). finux ventures into his own independent podcast about Tech, Security and general geekyness Speaking with people from the technology world about a whole host of different topics and subjects.
  • Hacker Public Radio (HPR) is an Internet Radio show (podcast) that releases shows every weekday Monday through Friday. HPR has a long lineage going back to Radio FreeK America, Binary Revolution Radio & Infonomicon, and it is a direct continuation of Twatech radio.
  • Linux in the Ham Shack is a podcast, a state of mind and a phenomenon. Originally begun to help amateur radio operators move from Microsoft and other closed-source operating systems and applications over to Linux and Free Software.
  • LinuxLugCast - We are an open Podcast/Lug that meets at 9:00 PM EST every first and third Friday of the month using Mumble. During the live show if you are unable to join us in mumble you can listen to us streaming live. You can also join us in IRC on freenode #LinuxLUGCast. We also have a Google Plus Community site and a mail list, visit our website for the direct links.
  • monsterb | daemons, gnus, and penguins. Oh my! is a link portal dedicated to BSD, Cyber Security, Emulation, Ethical Hacking, FOSS, GNU, illumos, Information Security, Infosec, IT Security, Linux, Podcasting, Programming, Python, Oggcasting, OpenSource, Scripting, Unix, and Video Games.
  • Xiph Wiki: In an effort to bring open-source ideals to the world of multimedia, the Xiph.Org Foundation develops a multitude of amazing products. This wiki describes our free and open protocols and software.
